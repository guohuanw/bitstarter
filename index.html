Your toddler loves to read book. Actually she loves to hear you to read the book to her. She will ask you to read the same book over and over until you fall into sleep. Your toddler loves to play your smartphone. It is so natural for her to use fingers to navigate. How about let READOLER record your book reading, shot a cover page picture and organize them on your smartphone. READOLER will turn your smartphone into a reading library. Your toddler will still "read" her real books, and It is still you read the book to your toddler, never bored.

READOLER is a open book reading library. You can share your book readings, rate how your toddler love each of your books. You can find out which books are loved the most by the READOLER community. There may be a better book reading version you can find and play for your toddler. What if your toddler love to read a book in Chinese and you have no idea how to read it to her? No problem, you can find one reading from READOLER.

FAQ

Will READOLER replace the physical books?
READOLER will not replace the physical books. Rather it is an extension to your physical book library. It is still the parents to decide how to read the book to your loved ones.
